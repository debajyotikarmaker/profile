Debajyoti Karmaker

I am a PhD candidate at The University of Queensland (UQ). I am currently working with Prof. Srinivasan in the Bio-Robotics lab at UQ. My research interests are in computer vision, machine learning, and deep learning. I am particularly interested in the areas of video understanding, bird action recognition, and biologically inspired collision avoidance strategies for quadcopters. Before starting my PhD, I was working as a lecturer at the American International University-Bangladesh (AIUB) - in the department of Computer Science. I also worked as a software engineer at Infra Blue Technology (IBT Games).
